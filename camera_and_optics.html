To achieve a microscopic-scale vision, a basic microscope was created by positioning a suitable optical lens and a CMOS camera at the opposite ends of a metal extension tube. The CMOS camera was then connected to a PC using the ESP32-CAM module. What can be observed when a generic die is placed under the microscope is shown in the figure (mettere foto con dimensioni).
